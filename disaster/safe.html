Safety Tips

Fire Safety

Ensure you have a fire extinguisher at home and know how to use it. In case of fire, stay low to avoid smoke inhalation and exit the building immediately.

Flood Preparedness

Know the flood risk in your area and have an emergency kit ready. Move to higher ground during a flood warning and avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.

Earthquake Safety

During an earthquake, drop, cover, and hold on. Stay indoors and away from windows. If outdoors, move to an open area away from buildings.

Severe Weather

Stay informed about weather conditions through official channels. Secure outdoor items, stay indoors, and avoid unnecessary travel during severe weather alerts.

Personal Safety

Be aware of your surroundings and avoid risky situations. Keep personal belongings secure and be cautious when interacting with strangers.

Cybersecurity

Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication on your accounts. Be wary of phishing scams and keep your software updated.

First Aid

Learn basic first aid skills and keep a first aid kit handy. Knowing how to respond to common injuries and emergencies can be crucial.

Travel Safety

Plan your travel route and inform someone of your whereabouts. Keep emergency contact numbers accessible and be cautious in unfamiliar areas.
